About

TED Club at Mackenzie is a club that hosts TED-inspired events to help students improve their public speaking skills and give them a stage to shine. Throughout the year, we plan to host numerous workshops and provide students opportunities to participate in speaking-orientated events and become better public speakers. We have done speech contests, student university panels from prestigious schools such as UOFT, Waterloo, and York, student-hosted TED Talks, and many more!
